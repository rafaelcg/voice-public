About VoxLibre

VoxLibre is a voice-powered translation app designed to help you communicate across language barriers. Simply speak in your native language, and VoxLibre will translate and speak your message in the language you need.

Perfect for daily conversations, navigating new places, or connecting with people who speak different languages.

Features

  • Voice Translation — Speak naturally and get instant translations
  • Multiple Languages — Support for many language pairs
  • Text-to-Speech — Hear translations spoken aloud
  • Simple & Intuitive — Easy to use for everyone

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does VoxLibre need microphone access?
    VoxLibre uses your microphone to listen to what you say and transcribe it for translation. We do not record or store your audio.
  • Is my data private?
    Yes. VoxLibre processes translations in real-time and does not store your personal conversations or voice recordings.
  • What languages are supported?
    VoxLibre supports a variety of languages. Check the app settings to see all available language options.
  • How do I change languages?
    Open the Settings tab in the app to select your source language (what you speak) and target language (what you want to translate to).
